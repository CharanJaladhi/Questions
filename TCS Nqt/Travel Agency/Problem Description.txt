Rank 'R', 1<=R<=5

Input is integers values from range 1 to 5 representing ranking should be taken from each of 5 residence for each of 5 travel destinations.

Output should be in format-
<String-Name of Destination>

<String-Name of Destination>
<String-Name of Destination>
